Iced Pour Over

Description

This iced pour-over coffee recipe is a refreshing take on the traditional pour-over method. Perfect for hot summer days, this coffee combines the rich, full flavors of a pour-over brew with the cool, refreshing taste of ice. Using a V60 dripper, this method ensures a clean and bright cup, with the ice rapidly cooling the hot coffee to preserve its delicate flavors and aromas.

Ingredients

  • 20 grams of coffee beans (medium grind)
  • 150 grams of ice
  • 150 grams of hot water (about 200°F or 93°C)

Steps

  1. Prepare Your Equipment:
    • Place the V60 dripper on top of your coffee server or carafe.
    • Put a V60 paper filter in the dripper and rinse it with hot water to remove any paper taste and to preheat the dripper and carafe. Discard the rinse water.
  2. Add Ice:
    • Place 150 grams of ice cubes into the coffee server or carafe.
  3. Grind the Coffee:
    • Grind 20 grams of coffee beans to a medium grind size, similar to sea salt.
  4. Add Coffee Grounds:
    • Pour the ground coffee into the rinsed filter in the V60 dripper. Gently shake the dripper to level the coffee bed.
  5. Pour the Hot Water:
    • Start your timer. Begin by pouring a small amount of hot water (about 40 grams) over the coffee grounds to bloom the coffee. This allows the coffee to degas and ensures even extraction.
    • Wait for 30 seconds.
  6. Continue Pouring:
    • After the bloom, slowly pour the remaining hot water (110 grams) in a circular motion, making sure to saturate all the coffee grounds. Aim to finish pouring by the 2-minute mark.
  7. Let the Coffee Brew:
    • Allow the coffee to drip through the filter and onto the ice in the server. The ice will rapidly cool the hot coffee, preserving its flavors and aromas.
  8. Serve and Enjoy:
    • Once all the water has passed through the coffee grounds (usually by around 2:30 to 3:00 minutes), remove the dripper.
    • Swirl the coffee in the server to mix and cool it evenly.
    • Pour the iced coffee into a glass filled with additional ice if desired.
    • Enjoy your refreshing iced pour-over coffee!
