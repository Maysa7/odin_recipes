Iced Pour Over

Description

This iced pour-over coffee recipe is a refreshing take on the traditional pour-over method. Perfect for hot summer days, this coffee combines the rich, full flavors of a pour-over brew with the cool, refreshing taste of ice. Using a V60 dripper, this method ensures a clean and bright cup, with the ice rapidly cooling the hot coffee to preserve its delicate flavors and aromas.

Ingredients

  • 20 grams of coffee beans (medium grind)
  • 150 grams of ice
  • 150 grams of hot water (about 200°F or 93°C)
